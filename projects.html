Grouper

Android application for collaborative task completion. Tasks are initiated by group members and can be assigned a location, user, and specific instruction. I designed and integrated NoSQL database for storage of user and group information. Integration of the Google Maps API was also necessary to give waypoints for individual tasks.

Zombify

A 2D survival game where players must persevere against waves of zombies with the power of guns and slow motion. I developed custom physics for the objects in game, such as parabolic jumping, vector-based bullet rotation and aiming, and slow motion traversal. It was insgightful to finally understand how one of my childhood pastimes is implemented.

Latarax Assistant

A Python based voice assistant that recognizes speech and executes commands accordingly. Local commands are available, such as opening launching apps like Spotify Google Chrome, as well as Wolfram Alpha queries for inputs that aren't recognized locally. To top it all off, at your service is a certain sith lord that asks for your commands.
